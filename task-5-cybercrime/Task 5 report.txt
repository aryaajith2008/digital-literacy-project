While researching cybercrime, I was surprised to learn how easily people can be tricked into UPI fraud through simple methods like fake calls and QR codes. Many users are unaware that receiving money does not require entering a PIN. This lack of awareness makes students and young users easy targets. I realized the importance of being cautious while handling online transactions. As a result, I will make sure never to share my personal banking details, OTPs, or UPI PIN with anyone. I will also verify links and avoid scanning unknown QR codes. This task helped me understand the importance of cybersecurity in everyday digital activities.
