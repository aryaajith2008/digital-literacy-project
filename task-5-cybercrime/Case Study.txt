Title: UPI Payment Fraud Case Study

UPI payment fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. It usually occurs when fraudsters trick users into sharing sensitive information or approving fraudulent transactions.

In a typical scenario, a student receives a phone call or message from a scammer pretending to be a bank official or seller. The scammer asks the victim to click on a link or scan a QR code to receive money. However, instead of receiving money, the victim unknowingly authorizes a payment. In some cases, fraudsters ask for OTPs or UPI PINs, which leads to unauthorized access to the victim’s bank account.

Students and young users are often targeted due to lack of awareness. The consequences include financial loss, stress, and misuse of personal data.

This type of fraud highlights the importance of digital awareness and cautious online behavior. Users must understand that receiving money never requires entering a PIN or scanning unknown QR codes.
