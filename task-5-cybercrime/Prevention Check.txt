Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes to receive money.
3. Verify links before clicking on them.
4. Use strong and unique passwords.
5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
6. Regularly check your bank transaction history.
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
8. Install apps only from trusted sources.
9. Immediately report fraud on https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 in case of emergency.
