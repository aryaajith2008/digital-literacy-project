Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The aim is to develop essential digital skills such as creating an online presence, practicing safe internet usage, understanding professional communication, and spreading awareness about cybercrime.

---

Task 1 – Infographic
Created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The infographic covers important topics such as digital tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

---

Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Created and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank. This task helped in building a basic professional online presence.

---

Task 3 – Platforms
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to spread digital literacy awareness.

---

Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails and created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage.

---

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Prepared a case study on UPI fraud and created a prevention checklist to help users stay safe online.
